I want to become an IT specialist in Kazakhstan. Where to go?

According to the Minister of Digital development of Bagdat Musin, annually Kazakhstan produces 20,000 IT specialists, and the demand for qualified information technology workers the last two years has grown by 55%. That's why IT education has become one of the most requested in Kazakhstan.

Almaty University of Energy and Communications named after Gumarbek Daukeev (AUES)

specialties informatics, information systems, computer engineering and software
teaching languages: Russian, Kazakh and English
Passing score: 55 points
tuition fee: first year - 750 thousand tenge, second year - 800 thousand tenge, third year - 850 thousand tenge, fourth - 900 thousand tenge
The university is divided into four institutes: electric power and electrical engineering, telecommunications and space engineering, thermal power engineering and control systems, information technology.

International University of Information Technologies (IITU)

specialties: big data analytics, cyber-physical computing, network and system administration, biocomputing, data science and others
teaching language: English
passing score: 60 points
tuition fee: for the first year - 1,104,000 tenge, for the second - 1,160,000 tenge, for the third - 1,218,000 tenge, for the fourth - 1,279,000 tenge
MUIT is the only university in Kazakhstan that has been accredited by ASIIN - the Agency for Accreditation of Educational Programs in Engineering, Informatics, Natural Sciences and Mathematics.

Astana IT University(AITU)

specialties: IT management, IT Entrepreneurship, Smart Technologies and others.
teaching language: English
passing score: 70 points
tuition fee: about 2 million tenge
AITU is an IT-only university. The entire teaching staff was trained in foreign universities. Teaching is conducted entirely in English.

Kazakh-British Technical University (KBTU)

specialties: automation and control, information systems, computers and software
teaching language: English
passing score: 70 points
cost per loan: 32 500 tenge
Education at KBTU is conducted in English, for admission to the university you will need to pass an internal exam. There is also a dual program. This means that the student will be able to close part of the educational loans by working in companies and gaining practical skills there.
